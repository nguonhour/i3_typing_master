About SpeedType

SpeedType is a free online typing test application designed to help you improve your typing speed and accuracy. Our mission is to provide a simple, effective tool for people of all skill levels to practice and measure their typing abilities.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SpeedType was created by a team of developers who recognized the importance of typing skills in today's digital world. Whether you're a student, professional, or just looking to improve your computer skills, we believe that efficient typing can save you time and boost your productivity.

How It Works

Our typing test measures your words per minute (WPM) and accuracy as you type provided texts. You can track your progress over time, challenge yourself with different difficulty levels, and compare your results with others.
